Here. I. Am.

This is me. I look like a very ordinary guy right? Actually, my friends would describe me as ‘rogue’, ‘weird’ and bold.

When I was growing up, I always felt like I had to fit into a mould. I always felt like I had to follow what everyone else did and try to be a ‘normal’ person. As a grew older however, I slowly began to find my own and now I am as unique and happy as ever!

Because I wanted to fit into a norm, I would try and like all of the same things as everyone else. The same sorts of music, the same sorts of tv shows and much more. Nowadays, I pride myself on constantly trying to push the boat out, to try new things and have new experiences. Along the way, I have found some great stuff and I want to share it with everyone so they can enjoy it too!

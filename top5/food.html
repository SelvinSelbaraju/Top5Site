Food Glorious Food

Ever since I was young, I would always spend time with my mum in the kitchen. At first, this was usually because I was hungry! But soon, I wanted to learn how to cook as good as she could.

Nowadays, I do a lot of cooking and like trying loads of different recipes. I mainly love Asian cuisine but also love Western comfort foods. With my Top 5 Foods, I wanted to give you something delicious yet simple to make yourself!

The Top 5

Drumroll please...

5

Coconut Bun

Although it would be difficult to eat an entire meal of just these, I felt like I had to include them on this list. The brioche-style bun is perfectly chewy and the buttery, coconut filling inside is so gorgeous and rich. They are difficult to make, so I would recommend buying them from Chinatown!

4

Sushi

I’m sure a lot of people have already had this but there are so many varieties. You can literally have anything as sushi nowadays. These are really fun to make yourself if you can get a hold of a bamboo roller, special sushi rice and seaweed.

3

Bánh Cuốn

You may have to search hard to find these in vietnamese restaurants. You usually need a dedicated chef! These rolls have a soft, chewy exterior with flavourful meat in the inside. These are usually served with special Vietnamese fish sauce which is tangy, spicy and a little sweet.

2

Katsu

Anyone who has ever had Japanese food, has probably had some sort of Katsu. For those who don’t know, Katsu is deep fried using Panko Breadcrumbs. These breadcrumbs are so crispy and so light. The original Katsu is Pork, but Chicken, Tofu and Prawn are also delicious!

1

Hainanese Chicken Rice

This is my number one food of all time. There is never really a time I wouldn’t want to eat these. It’s beautifully steamed chicken in a perfect blend of garlic, shallots and ginger. The meat just comes off the bone and the rice is cooked using the stock of the chicken. A must-try

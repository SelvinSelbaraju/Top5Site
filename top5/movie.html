Food Glorious Food

Ever since I was young, I would always spend time with my mum in the kitchen. At first, this was usually because I was hungry! But soon, I wanted to learn how to cook as good as she could.

Nowadays, I do a lot of cooking and like trying loads of different recipes. I mainly love Asian cuisine but also love Western comfort foods. With my Top 5 Foods, I wanted to give you something delicious yet simple to make yourself!

The Top 5

Drumroll please...

5

Katsu

Anyone who has ever had Japanese food, has probably had some sort of Katsu. For those who don’t know, Katsu is deep fried using Panko Breadcrumbs. These breadcrumbs are so crispy and so light. The original Katsu is Pork, but Chicken, Tofu and Prawn are also delicious!

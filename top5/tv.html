Chillout and Binge

As I’ve grown older, I have found myself unable to binge watch like I used to. However, I do still love putting my feet up in front and watching TV at the end of a long, hard day of work.

I’ve got a variety of genres down below. I think all of these shows mean something special to me in many different ways. Even thinking of these shows often invokes certain emotions in me due to the memories I have of them.

The Top 5

Drumroll please...

5

Suits

Flashy, clever and dramatic. Suits is a great show that unfortunately falls short as the series goes on. I would say it peaks mid-way through where it is so entertaining. It’s funny and has a great storyline. It may inspire you to be a lawyer too!

4

Friends

Friends is one of those shows where no matter what episode is on, you can just sit down and watch it. It’s that easy and enjoyable to watch. An absolute classic that so many people know. Great characters, great chemistry and fun!

3

Friday Night Lights

This show follows an American Football team at a high school in Texas. The show really pulled on the heart strings as it followed the lives of the teenagers and their parents. The American Football scenes were really cool too and inspired me to want to play it.

2

Game of Thrones

Most people have already watched this, but it had to be mentioned. The show was absolutely phenomental throughout until maybe the final season. Some people hated it but I didn’t think it was so bad. The fighting, the music, the storyline. It was all so great in Game of Thrones.

1

How I Met Your Mother

Some people think this is just a copy of Friends, but I actually think it’s slightly better. I found HIMYM funnier and it still had a great story. I actually cried when I watched the final episode, which is something I usually do not do. It’s so easy to watch and the characters are great.

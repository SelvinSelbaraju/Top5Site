Discover a New Art Form

I first started reading manga when I had swimming lessons in primary school. I would go to the library and borrow all sorts of different manga. I thought the art was beautiful and the stories funny.

I then started watching anime in Year 10 when a friend introduced me to it. Ever since then, I’m been quite hooked and I would say I watch more Anime than Western TV shows! I then started reading manga again last year after a friend won a competition!

The Top 5

Drumroll please...

5

Boku no Hero Academia

In a world full of superpowered individuals, it must suck to be a person without a power right? The characters are so diverse with people having a wide range of awesome powers. The music is beautiful and watching the characters evolve is amazing.

4

Death Note

Death Note is so special because of how unique it is. It is the only one in this list that doesn’t directly involve fighting. Follow Light Yagami as he tries to rid the world of all criminal activity, by killing all criminals. I read this one, and the story is incredible. The best story on this list.

3

Shingeki no Kyojin

One of the most popular Anime/Manga at the moment, and for very good reason. The anime is dark. Very dark. The fighting, the hype and the characters are so well designed and the really change over time. This was the first anime I watched and now it’s in its final season. Really emotional.

2

Hunter X Hunter

I haven’t even finished this show yet but I had to include it on the list. The concept of the show is so cool. You have to watch it for yourself. In addition, the character development is out of this world. The show may look innocent, but it is very dark in its own way. Awesome music too.

1

Kimetsu no Yaiba

It was so hard to pick my favourite but it had to be this one. The art in the Anime is absolutely phonomenal. The colours pop like crazy. The music is so beautiful as well. There are some awesome characters that really stand out too along with amazing hype moments. This is just so good.
